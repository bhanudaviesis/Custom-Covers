What fabric do you use for marine covers?
We use Sunbrella acrylic and heavy-duty polyester canvas. Both are UV-stabilised, water-resistant, and proven for marine environments.
Can you make covers for RIBs?
Yes — RIBs, inflatables, and tenders all welcome. Their unusual profiles are something we're experienced with.
Do you fit tie-down straps and buckles?
All marine covers include appropriate fastening systems — we'll discuss the right option for your storage setup during the quote process.
